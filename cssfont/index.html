The style of this type is inspired from retro themes in the 80s and 90s. I really like the look of bright colors, which is why I used neon blue and neon pink. There was also some inspirtation from Cyperpunk fonts and themes. One of my favorite games is Tetris, thus the reason why the type looks "blocky". Overall, I wanted to create a style that uses both Cyperpunk themes and 90s logos.
